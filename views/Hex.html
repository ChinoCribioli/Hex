Hex Game

The Hex is a game for two players, red and blue, that take turns to paint the tiles of the following board.
As the red player, your goal is to connect the red horizontal bars that lie above and below the board with a path of red tiles.
As the blue player, the goal of the machine is to avoid it.
To start, click on the tile you want to color first, and wait for the machine to respond with a blue tile.
If you want the machine to start, click on the "Play second" button.
